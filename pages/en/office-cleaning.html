Professional office cleaning

Clean offices mean a more productive team and a stronger professional image. AlloPropreté offers tailored cleaning contracts for businesses of every size.

We understand that each business has specific needs. That's why we offer flexible plans: from daily cleaning for large open-plan offices to weekly interventions for smaller teams. Our crews operate at hours that suit you, without disrupting your activity.

What we clean

Workstations: desks, screens, keyboards, phones
Floors: carpet vacuuming, tile/hardwood mopping
Restrooms: daily cleaning and disinfection
Common areas: kitchen, meeting rooms, reception
Internal windows and glass partitions
