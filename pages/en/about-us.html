Our story

Cleaning has been our craft since 2014

AlloPropreté was born from a simple observation: finding a reliable, professional cleaning provider is often a struggle. Vague quotes, rotating teams, inconsistent results... We set out to change that.

Our mission: offer every client, whether an individual or a business, a flawless cleaning service with transparent pricing and a dedicated point of contact.

Today we operate across France with a team of trained, equipped professionals. Our growth is built on one principle: every satisfied customer is our best advertisement.

Our values

Excellence
